# Selfhost Upgrading Instructions

## How to use these instructions

Check your existing docker-compose.yaml and look at the top for the current version's revision date.

Then, follow every step outlined here for _each of the revision dates_ newer than your current version, all the way until the newest version.

Some versions may be omitted here if they do not require particular upgrade steps themselves.

## Migrating to 2025-12-15

No steps are needed when migrating to this version.
